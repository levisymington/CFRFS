Training Modules

Bush Firefighter 

The NSW Rural Fire Service Bush Firefighter (2014) qualification (BF) is an internal NSW RFS qualification to provide members with the level of competency needed to fight bush and grass fires safely and effectively, under supervision.

Village Firefighter

The NSW Rural Fire Service Village Firefighter 2014 qualification; or VF, is an internal NSW RFS qualification to provide members with the level of competency needed to work safely under supervision as part of team of firefighters using relevant equipment and communications to control fires in village environments safely and effectively

Advanced Firefighter

Training courses which provide intermediate through to advanced skills and knowledge in preparation for working at an advanced level without direct supervision within a team or crew.

Crew Leader

Training courses which provide highly advanced skills and knowledge in preparation for taking command or control of large operations, planning and/or leading multiple teams or crews.

Specialist Skills

Unique and specialist skills that compliment a Brigade. Skills such as Driving, Chainsaw operations, Advnaced First Aid, Aircraft Marshalling.

Out of Zone / District Deployments

Handy guides and reocmmendations that will assist in the preparation oand undertaking Deployments as an individual.
