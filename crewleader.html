DIFFERENT SKILLS OF A CREW LEADER

CREW LEADER WILDFIRE

(CLW)

Crew Leader Wildfire is an internal program designed to enable suitable members to supervise a crew at a
wildfire incident. 

The pre-requisite for CLW is:
Qualified AF

CREW LEADER VILLAGE

(CLV)

Crew Leader Village is an internal program designed to enable suitable members to supervise a crew at village
type incidents. 

The pre-requisite for CLV is: Qualified AF and VF

CREW LEADER SUPERVISION

(CLS)

Crew Leader Supervision is an internal program designed to enable suitable members to:
 Control a small incident,
 Command a small SECTOR,
 Lead a Strike Team or a Task Force
 Provide general leadership and supervisory skills. 

The pre-requisite for CLS is:
Qualified CLW

(FILE) RFS Radio Comminications SOP (2012)

The Communications Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) prescribe standardised radio communications for use throughout the NSW Rural Fire Service (NSW RFS). The SOPs establish the radio networks used by the NSW RFS as Strategic, Area, Command, Tactical and Task networks. These SOPs should be read and used in conjunction with all other NSW RFS Policies, Service Standards, Operational / Incident Management Procedures(OMP / IMPs) , the Firefighters Pocket Book and Training Manuals.

(FILE) Wildfire Behaviour Workbook (2014)

This manual aims to provide members with the understanding and tools required to make informed decisions with regard to active fire behaviour or the potential of a fire and its behaviour should one start. It is also intended to be a reference manual for firefighters now and into the future.
