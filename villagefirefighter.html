VILLAGE FIREFIGHTER

VF is an extension of Bush Firefighter (BF) training to cover village typefires/incidents. Only firefighters who are expected to attend village typefires/incidents need training to VF level. Both BF and VF can be considered as “basic” or “recruit” level programs.
